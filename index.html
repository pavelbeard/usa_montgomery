Jr. Counselors - R03011

This program offers participants an opportunity to learn how to plan, teach and execute youth programs and activities. Junior Counselors will work in Summer Fun Centers, and shadow experienced camp counselors while developing these skills. The Junior counselor program is designed to create leaders/role models and prepare participants to become effective and talented counselors. Junior Counselors are provided with camp t-shirts, appropriate training, and a program manual.

Junior counselors are only allowed to register for a maximum of two sessions.

A parent or guardian must complete a camper information form for each Junior counselor.

All Junior Counselors must attend one of the three mandatory training courses prior to serving in this role. Available training dates and locations will be emailed to participants.
